Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on the Job Portal?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the page, fill in your details (name, email, and password), and verify your email address. Once verified, your account will be ready to use.

Is the job portal free for job seekers?

Yes, our job portal is completely free for job seekers. You can create an account, search for jobs, and apply without any charges.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to set a new password.

How can I search for jobs on the portal?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to enter keywords, job titles, or company names. You can also filter results by location, job type, or salary range to find the most relevant opportunities.

How do I apply for a job?

Once you find a job you're interested in, click on the "Apply Now" button. You can either upload your resume or fill out the application form directly. Make sure your profile is up-to-date before submitting your application.
